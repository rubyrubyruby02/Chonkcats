What is a chonk cat?

Chonky? Internet-speak for chunky. The “chonk scale” became a meme in 2018, with “chonkness” measured on a BMI chart that went from “A fine boi” to “Oh Lord He Comin'”. Read more about the definition of chonky





Is my cat a chonk cat? A few clues...

There are a variety of classifications for these pawsitively plump chonks based on girth. Chonk is a way of life and here are a few clues your cat has embraced it:

  1. The perr-suit of simple pleasures
  2. Happiest at home
  3. Nap seekers
  4. Food motivated


Chonk Categories

A fine boi

Characterized by a relatively low chub factor, a fine boi has chonk potential but it has not yet been fully realized

He chonk

Now we're getting somewhere. A little bit of cushioning making the he chonk the purrrfect cuddle buddy.

A heckin chonker

I can haz snakcs? The heftychonk is food motivated and will work for treats. But, you know, not too hard.

Heftychonk

Added insulation makes the heftychonk challenging to lift but a delight to take a catnap with.

Megachonker

The general philosophy of the megachonker is this: "Lay off me, I'm starving".

Oh Lord he comin'

Watch out, world! The roly-poly may be tender but runs the risk of breaking your lap. Do not get in his or her way when it's feedin' time.
